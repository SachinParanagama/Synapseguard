Our Product

What is Synapseguard..?

Synapseguard is a groundbreaking approach which is introduced to the medical industry to support the early diagnosis of schizophrenia among the public. As schizophrenia is a chronic mental, we understand the importance of early diagnosis and effective treatment of this disease in the first place which will help both the individual and the people associated with him. Synapseguard is your dedicated healthcare companion, utilizing cutting-edge technology address four key areas: Schizophrenia Cognition Assessment, Voice Emotion-Based Schizophrenia Likelihood Recognition, Emotion-Based Schizophrenia Likelihood Recognition and Questionnaire Based Schizophrenia Likelihood Recognition.

With combination of above mentioned four sections, Synapseguard keeps a watchful eye on your interactions, by covering the main symptoms of the schizophrenia such as hallucination, delusion, emotion and vocal flat affect, attentiveness, memorizing and decision-making ability. From this our system provide a clear idea about the schizophrenia likelihood of the user to the medical expertise, making it easier for the early diagnosis process.

At Synapseguard, our vision is to empower individual's mental well-being in the digital age. We offer practical solutions that integrate seamlessly into the topic of early diagnosis, fostering better health with achieving high detection accuracies. Join us in this transformative journey as we redefine the way people experience their digital lives, creating a future where well-being and technology coexist harmoniously.
